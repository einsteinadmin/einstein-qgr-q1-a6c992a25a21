How the Weeks Work

Weeks 1, 2, 3, 5, 6
Single round-robin inside each conference. Most teams play in-conference; a couple of cross games sprinkle in.
Week 4 — Cross Showdown
Every team plays a cross-conference opponent. CTXH vs DFWT+Tampa, all 5 matchups, same week.
Week 6 — Rivalry Week
The biggest in-conference grudge matches plus one cross-conference rivalry. Bragging rights on the line.
Weeks 7 + 8 — Playoffs
Top 2 per conference advance. W7 = conference championships. W8 = national title + 3rd place game.

  • 5 cards per team — one per scoring category, for the whole regular season.
  • +50% boost in whichever category you apply the card to that week.
  • One card max per week. You can't stack multiple cards on a single game.
  • Pick your spots. Five cards across six regular-season weeks means one week you'll play none — make it count.

5The Scoreboard App
